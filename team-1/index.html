About Us

HYT Foundation exists to inspire and aid young entrepreneurs to attain their full potential. Our purpose is to bring the next generation forward – establishing a future with a society of young generation leaders.

Our Vision

Establish a competent society of young professional leaders and entrepreneurs through honing their vocational aptitude with the means of advanced technology, innovating creations to serve the world.

Our Mission

Guiding young and aspiring generation leaders by professionally mentoring them, developing a social responsibility and empowering them to pass it forward to future generations while growing internationally

OUR VALUES

Why we do it

Our goal is to bring forth knowledgeable young entrepreneurs and cater the world’s needs through these exemplary leaders while adapting and understanding generational differences by being flexible and innovative. Future global innovations are driven by the burning passion of these young people and their desire to succeed, all the while aiding them to obtain financial independence.

Who are we

We are the foundation for future generation leaders in which we support to enlighten and hone the youth in acquiring an adept set of business skills, professionally mentoring young adults and directing them to pursue what they aspire to become.

Why should you join us?

To become outstanding young aspiring leaders and entrepreneurs, the HYT Foundation Inc. provides opportunities for young people to learn and enhance their abilities.

Who are we helping?

The HYT Foundation Inc. is assisting future generations in accomplishing goals, by providing professional mentoring to emerging professional leaders and entrepreneurs. To motivate and pass on their knowledge and skills to future generations leaders.
